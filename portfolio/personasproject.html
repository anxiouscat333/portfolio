Personas and Storyboarding

Part I: Context/Problem

Due to background differences, I identified that most international students around me couldn't figure out the proper way to use the American laundry machines provided at Brown. In my research I try to identify the differences between the machines and what they're used to back home and I attempt to standardize the interface such that anyone can understand how to use the machines regardless of their background.
